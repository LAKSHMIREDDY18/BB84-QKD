1. Introduction to QKD & BB84

Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) leverages principles of quantum mechanics to establish a secure shared secret key between two parties (Alice and Bob) over an insecure channel, potentially monitored by an eavesdropper (Eve).

The BB84 protocol is the pioneering and most well-known QKD protocol.
